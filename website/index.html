What is DogeRocket?

DogeRocket is a sophisticated DeFi protocol on the Polygon network, integrating meme-inspired elements with advanced financial mechanisms. It features fast, cost-effective transactions, a 2% unstake fee that bolsters the rewards pool, and high-yield staking up to 300% APY through our Rocket Booster system. Empowered by DogeDAO, it places governance in the hands of the community, catering to both short-term traders and long-term investors seeking sustainable growth.

DogeRocket Tokenomics

1 Billion DRKT Supply: Designed for Sustainability

Our tokenomics framework prioritizes fairness, long-term viability, and community incentives:

  • 10% (100M DRKT): Allocated to DogeDAO at Token Generation Event (TGE) for IDO, liquidity pools, airdrops, and marketing initiatives.
  • 40% (400M DRKT): Dedicated to staking rewards, enhanced by the 2% unstake fee directed to the rewards pool for continuous yield generation.
  • 30% (300M DRKT): Locked in the rewards pool, vested over 10 periods under DogeDAO governance following the IDO.
  • 20% (200M DRKT): Vested and claimable by DogeDAO over 10 years to support liquidity additions, airdrops, marketing, and treasury management.
